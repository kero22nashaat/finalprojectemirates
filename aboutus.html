About us

Overview

V A H CONTRACTING CO. L.L.C has established in the year 2011 in Dubai as the civil contractors and can execute all type of civil works such as construction of luxurious Villas, Residential, Commercial and Industrial Buildings in Dubai.

At present the Company holds B+G+1 License in Dubai and is also in the process of upgrading the same within short period.

The company is leading by Mr.Harish V.Amin who is having a very vast experience & dedication in construction & technology for providing high quality building services who has executed so many projects in the Middle East country and has carried out many construction projects in Dubai which include Luxury Villas, Group Villas, Residential, Industrial Buildings and High rise buildings.

We also set our focus on MEP solutions including Design, supply, installation, testing and commissioning of complete MEP systems that include Electrical, Plumbing, Drainage, Fire Alarm, Firefighting, Low Current, HVAC as per the requirement of the local authorities of the U.A.E for commercial and residential buildings, villas, hospital, shopping malls, high rise towers, Infrastructure and Industrial Projects. We use innovative construction methods and problemsolving approaches to respond uniquely and specifically to each customer’s needs.

Our team includes dedicated and qualified task forces for Engineers, Foremen and Office staffs. Its Senior Project Manager has over 18 years’ experience in Dubai with all types of construction works and capable of Planning, Executing and Handling different types of projects.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to deliver professional construction services based on quality craftsmanship in a very timely manner. And also to provide creative and distinctive design solutions for the most complex and challenging demands in the construction industry.

We are dedicated to providing thoroughly professional service with an extra personal touch and sensitivity to the design criteria.

Our varied core competencies allow us to move a project swiftly from an idea on an engineering drafting table to a realized luxury villas, commercial & residential buildings.
